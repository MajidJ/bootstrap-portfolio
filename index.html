About Me

Hello! My name is Majid Jamaleldine. I’ve travelled to 27 countries so far and enjoy spending time with my family members living all around the world. My academic focus is centered deep within the sciences but I've always had a passion for the arts.

I am currently a partner at Line Break Media, LLC. in Minneapolis, Minnesota and have spent the past year building out the motion graphics capabilities of the company. In March 2018 I will be transitioning to a full time focus on my full stack development academics.
